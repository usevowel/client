## Why This Pricing Model?

### Standard Connector App Pattern

This is how most successful Shopify integrations work:

**Similar Apps:**
- **Mailchimp** - Free connector, external platform billing
- **Klaviyo** - Free connector, platform subscription
- **Google Analytics** - Free connector, premium external
- **Zendesk** - Free connector, support billing

### Benefits for You

**1. Direct Relationships**
- Pay Vowel directly (no Shopify middleman fees)
- Pay Google directly (no markup)
- Better customer support
- More flexible terms

**2. Start Free**
- Shopify app: **Always FREE**
- Vowel: **Free tier available**
- Google: **Free tier available**
- Test with real customers at $0

**3. Scale Flexibly**
- Pay only for what you use
- Upgrade when you're ready
- No long-term contracts
- Cancel anytime

**4. Full Control**
- Monitor usage in real-time
- Set budget caps
- Upgrade/downgrade instantly
- Transparent costs

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much will this actually cost me?

**Shopify App:** $0 (always free)

**Vowel + Google:** Depends on your usage. Many stores start on entirely free tiers and upgrade as they grow. Visit [vowel.to/pricing](https://vowel.to/pricing) to see plans and calculate your estimated costs.

### Can I start completely free?

**Yes!** Both Vowel and Google offer free tiers. You can test with real customers without spending anything.

### What if I exceed free tiers?

You'll be prompted to upgrade on the Vowel platform or automatically billed by Google for additional usage. You can set spending caps to control costs.

### How is billing handled?

- **Shopify App:** No billing - it's free
- **Vowel Platform:** Direct at vowel.to (credit card or PayPal)
- **Google Gemini:** Through Google Cloud billing

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes - no contracts, no cancellation fees. The Shopify app stays installed (free), and you can pause/cancel your Vowel and Google accounts separately.

### Are there discounts available?

Vowel offers various discounts for non-profits, annual prepay, new stores, and startups. Check [vowel.to/pricing](https://vowel.to/pricing) for current offers.

### Do I need a credit card to start?

**No** - You can start with free tiers:
- Vowel free tier: No credit card required
- Google free tier: Google account only
- Shopify app: Always free
