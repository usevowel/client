### 💰 Pricing Structure Documented

**Shopify Connector App:**
- FREE - No installation fees or monthly charges

**External Services Required:**

**Vowel Platform** (vowel.to):
- Free tier available
- Paid plans for higher usage
- See vowel.to/pricing for current details

**Google Gemini API:**
- Free tier available (60 requests/min)
- Pay-per-use beyond free tier
- Typical cost: $0-10/month

**Total Cost Approach:**
- Can start at $0/month with free tiers
- Scales with usage
- Direct billing (no Shopify middleman)
