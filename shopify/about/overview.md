# Vowel Voice Assistant for Shopify

## Tagline

**Add voice shopping to your Shopify store in minutes.**

## One-Liner

Vowel for Shopify is a voice-powered AI assistant that lets your customers shop hands-free using natural voice commands - no coding required.

## Problem Statement

Online shopping friction costs conversions:
- Customers struggle with complex navigation and search
- Mobile typing is frustrating and slow
- Product discovery takes too long
- Cart abandonment rates remain high
- Accessibility barriers limit audience reach
- Support costs continue to rise

Traditional Shopify stores rely on clicking, typing, and scrolling - but customers want faster, more natural ways to shop.

## Solution

Vowel adds an AI-powered voice assistant to your Shopify store that:
- ✅ **Installs in 2 minutes** - One-click from Shopify App Store
- ✅ **No coding required** - Works with any theme automatically
- ✅ **Natural voice shopping** - Customers speak, AI acts
- ✅ **Hands-free browsing** - Perfect for mobile shoppers
- ✅ **Smart product discovery** - Voice-powered search
- ✅ **Increased conversions** - Faster path to purchase

## What It Does

Your customers can now interact with your store using voice:

**Product Search**
- "Find wireless headphones under $100"
- "Show me red dresses in size medium"
- "What's your best-selling product?"

**Navigation**
- "Go to the sale section"
- "Show me new arrivals"
- "Take me to checkout"

**Shopping Actions**
- "Add this to my cart"
- "What's in my cart?"
- "Apply my discount code"

**Store Information**
- "What are your shipping times?"
- "Do you have a return policy?"
- "What's your phone number?"

## Get Started

### For Merchants
1. **Install from Shopify App Store** (Coming Soon)
   - One-click installation
   - No technical setup required
   - Works instantly with your theme

2. **Get Your API Key** (Free)
   - Sign up at [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/)
   - Get free Gemini API key
   - Free tier: 60 requests/minute

3. **Configure & Launch**
   - Add to your theme in customizer
   - Enter your Vowel App ID and API key
   - Choose button position
   - Go live!
